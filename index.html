ABOUT BUNI HUB

Buni Innovation Hub under Tanzania Commission for Science and Technology (COSTECH) focuses on coordinating and supporting the Innovation ecosystem in Tanzania as part of COSTECH’s mandate to promote and coordinate Science, Technology and Innovation in Tanzania.

Buni coordinates and supports the Innovation Ecosystem through various activities, including: Building Capacity to Hub Managers (New and Existing); Mentoring Innovation Spaces; Support establishment of Innovation Spaces in Higher learning Institutions, Research &Development Institutions (R&D) and Local Government Agencies; Connect and act as Intermediary between Innovation Spaces and government, Support Innovators and STARTUPS at early stage, and advice COSTECH about the Innovation ecosystem in Tanzania.

We work with partners and beneficiaries to make sure our role is impactful and deliver according to needs from the enablers of the Innovation Ecosystem.

Our Goals

Our goal is to physique impactful Technology, Entrepreneurship and Innovation enablers and leaders (especially in Innovation Spaces) who will support and lead Innovators/STARTUPS to Innovate and solve challenges which are facing our Communities. Also identifying and supporting STARTUPS/Innovators at very early stage

This involves establishment of preconditions that will motivate mutual interaction/collaboration at the University spaces depending on the available opportunities. Gender Equality in Innovation, tech and Entrepreneurship

Buni is dedicated to ensuring gender equality in Innovation. This includes bridging the gender gap for tech-preneurs, ensuring increased involvement of Women in Technology and Innovation in general. Buni does this under the Buni Divaz program, supporting women led startups and events, as well as through the Annual Women in Tech Festival.
